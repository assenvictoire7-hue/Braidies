Braidies is a website I built focused on hair care and hairstyling, specifically braiding.
The goal of this project is to teach people how to braid different hairstyles, including popular ones like cornrows and twists. 
On the website, you will also find helpful advice on how to properly take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.

To make learning easy and enjoyable, Braidies is organized into different sections.
You can easily swap or move from one section to another without any confusion.
Whether you are a beginner or someone looking to improve your braiding skills,
the website guides you step by step through each style.
Braidies is my way of sharing the art of braiding and encouraging good hair care habits for everyone.
