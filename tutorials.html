Braid Tutorials

Welcome to our step-by-step braid tutorials! Whether you are a beginner or improving your skills,this detail guides will help you braid with confidence.

Before you start

Before Braiding your hair,it is important to prepare it properly.Clean and well-prepared hair makes braiding easier and gives a neat,long lasting result.

  • Wash and condition your hair
  • Detangle using a combo and brush
  • Moisturize your hair with oil and cream
  • Section your hair neatly using a combo

How to Do a basic three stran braid

  1. Start by combining your hair to remove tangles
  2. Divide the hair into three equal sections
  3. Hold the right section in your right hand and the left in your left hand
  4. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  5. Continue altenating until you reach the end
  6. Secure the hair with a hair band

How to do conrows

  1. Use a combo to divide the hair into straight sections
  2. Start at the front of one section .
  3. Divide a small portion into three parts
  4. Begin braiding while adding a small amount of hair as you move backward
  5. Keep the braod close to the scalp
  6. Continue to the end, then finish with a regular braid.

Tips:Keep your hands firm but not too tight to avoid pain or damge
