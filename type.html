Types of Braid styles

Explore different hairstyles and find perfect look for you.

1. Box braids

Box braids are one of the most popular styles.They are created by divivding the hair into small square sections and braiding each sections. They are stylish, long lasting and easy to maintain

2.Conrows

Conrows are braided close to the scalp in straight lines or creative patterns. There are neat ,elegant and both casual and formal look.

Ghana Braids

Ghana Braids are thicker and raised braids that often follow beautiful patterns . The are bold, protective and very fashionable.

Knotless braids

Knotless braids are modern versions of box braids . They are lighther , more natural looking, and cause less tension on the scalp.

Twist braids

Twist braids are created by twisting two strands of hair instead of three. They are simple soft and give a different texture compare to regular braids

Which style is best for you?

The best braid style depends on yoyr hair type,lifestyle, and personal state. Try different styles and discover what makes you feel confident and beautiful.
